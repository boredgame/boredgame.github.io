The games we play

Upcoming Games

I downloaded and tested the following games, so we can start playing basically any time we like.

I am using a framework called Tabletop Simulator which is basically just a physics engine designed for boardgames. There is an amazing modding community supporting this framework, which meanes after you have the engine, almost all of the more commonly known boardgames are downloadable for free!

And this means of course that we can also create our own games pretty easily.

Secret Hitler

Secret Hitler is a hidden role game featring political intrigue and betrayal set in 1930s Germany.

45 min easy social

Codenames

Two rival spymasters know the secret identities of 25 agents. Their teammates know the agents only by their codenames.

15 min easy social

London

Tasked with rebuilding London in the decades following the great fire, players juggle building requirements, bank loans, and poverty as they strive to realize their vision for the city.

60-90 min moderate competetive

One Night Ultimate Werewolf

The classic Werewolf game we all know and love. No moderator, no elimination, ten-minute games.

10 min easy social

Pandemic

In Pandemic, several virulent diseases have broken out simultaneously all over the world! The players are disease-fighting specialists whose mission is to treat disease hotspots while researching cures for each of four plagues before they get out of hand.

45 min moderate cooperative

Kingdomino

In Kingdomino, you are a Lord seeking new lands in which to expand your kingdom. Available with two different rulesets: Vanilla and Hardcore (aka Milano) style.

15-20 min easy competetive

Root

The nefarious Marquise de Cat has seized the great woodland, intent on harvesting its riches. Under her rule, the many creatures of the forest have banded together. This Alliance will seek to strengthen its resources and subvert the rule of Cats.

60-90 min difficult competetive

The Grizzled

The Grizzled is a cooperative game about survival in the trenches during the first World War where players win or lose together.

10 min easy cooperative

Concept

Activity for people who can't draw, act, or explain things.

40 min easy social

Cards Against Humanity

For you horrible, horrible people.

30 min easy social

Century: Spice Road

In Century: Spice Road, players are caravan leaders who travel the famed silk road to deliver spices to the far reaches of the continent for fame and glory.

30-45 min moderate competetive

Chinatown

This is a negotiation game in the truest sense of the word. Your task is to trade and grow your businesses in the 60's Chinatown.

60 min moderate competetive

Santorini

Santorini is an accessible strategy game, simple enough for an elementary school classroom while aiming to provide gameplay depth and content for hardcore gamers to explore.

20 min easy competetive

Magic Maze

A co-op game where each player has a very limited set of moves, complicated by the fact that they aren't even allowed to talk!

15 min easy cooperative

Buregle Bros.

Burgle Bros. is a cooperative game for 1-­4 players. Players are unique members of a crew trying to pull off a robbery of a highly secure building — without getting caught.

54-90 min moderate cooperative

Spirit Island

Spirit Island is a complex and thematic cooperative game about defending your island home from colonizing Invaders.

90-120 min difficult cooperative

Azul

As a tile-laying artist, you have been challenged to embellish the walls of the Royal Palace of Evora.

30-45 min moderate competetive
